Brave & Berserk

10x23'
All Ages
Limited-run live-action narration/animated children’s series exploring how twin puppies navigate their owners traumas and life in two worlds.

Brave and Berserk are rez mutts adopted at birth by an Anishinabe and British-Canadian LGBTQ2S+ couple. We experience life through the eyes of the puppies going back and forth between the bush and the city. Brave is a foil for the Anishinabe experience and Berserk for the British-Canadian, both being terms for warriors in their respective cultures. The problems of their owners may be out of their control, but how they react differently to each experience helps shape a better understanding and bond between the two. Based on a true story from the lives of Dude and Chester.

Funky Hair
12x25'
8-12 years
Live action at the hair studio + gaming lounge

At the age of 12, Lucas is already facing a very harsh reality – the eminent passing of his father. In his last moments, Lucas’ dad asks him to help his mom financially by opening how town’s first LGBTQ2S+ amonia free hairdresser shop. With a small loan and a big dream, Lucas opens his doors to town misfits and lets them have their personality shine through with wacky hairdoes. While new styles are being made, and some frantic hair mishaps bubbling, sit down, relax, and game while you wait --- because after all Lucas is but a kid and this is his fun hair lounge. Based on a true story

About Elizabeth Ladouceur

Having a mixed identity in all the senses, Canadian-Mexican content creator Elizabeth Ladouceur dreamt of a cross platform game & storyworld where one could delve into the fears and monsters within. urMonster content has things for all age groups, from pre-school to Tweens --- each piece connecting as you play/watch on. Mixing storytelling with kid driven narratives with a focus on family connections and mental health, Elizabeth uses her clinical research experience and TV experience to bring stories from a blend of apps, stop animation, animation, VFX, and live action. Play on, and discover the beauty that's in you.
